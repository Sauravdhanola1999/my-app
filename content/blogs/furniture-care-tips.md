Furniture is a significant investment in your home. Proper care and maintenance can extend its lifespan and keep it looking beautiful for years to come.

## Daily Care Tips

### Regular Cleaning
- Dust your furniture regularly with a soft, dry cloth
- Use appropriate cleaners for different materials
- Avoid harsh chemicals that can damage finishes
- Clean spills immediately to prevent stains

### Protection from Elements
- Keep furniture away from direct sunlight to prevent fading
- Maintain proper humidity levels in your home
- Use coasters and placemats to protect surfaces
- Rotate cushions regularly for even wear

## Material-Specific Care

### Wood Furniture
- Polish wood furniture every 3-6 months
- Use wood-specific cleaners and polishes
- Avoid placing hot items directly on wood surfaces
- Protect from scratches with felt pads

### Upholstered Furniture
- Vacuum regularly to remove dust and debris
- Treat stains immediately with appropriate cleaners
- Rotate and flip cushions for even wear
- Consider professional deep cleaning annually

### Leather Furniture
- Clean with leather-specific products
- Condition leather regularly to prevent cracking
- Keep away from direct heat sources
- Use leather protectors to prevent stains
